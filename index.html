zkSNACKs was a pioneering company founded in 2018 to enhance Bitcoin's privacy and fungibility through the innovative Wasabi Wallet. After achieving groundbreaking success, the company shut down its coinjoin coordinator in June 2024, marking the conclusion of its mission and leaving behind a legacy of empowering users with greater financial freedom and a lasting impact on the Bitcoin ecosystem. Sincere gratitude goes to all contributors, supporters, and users who made this journey possible and continue to advance the cause of financial freedom and privacy.
